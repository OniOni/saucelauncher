You have two options to open the Sauce Launcher sidebar:
  1. On a Mac press: "Cmd + Shift + L"
  2. Use the Firefox sidebar menu, at the top of the screen between 'Edit' and 'History', click 'View'.
    • - Next click 'Sidebar'
    • - Finally click 'Sauce Launcher'
